2nd INTERNATIONAL ONLINE CONFERENCE ON
"ADVANCEMENTS IN SUSTAINABLE MATERIALS AND INFRASTRUCTURE"(ASMI-2023)

CALL FOR PAPER BROCHURE

CALL FOR PAPER

The second international conference on "The Advancements in Sustainable Materials and infrastructure" (ASMI-2023) invites high quality research papers in the domain of civil & Environmental Engineering.The primary Objective is to bring academicians, technocrats,engineers,researchers and students to a common platform to share their latest research findings,experiences,and insights in the field of Sustainable materials and infrastructure.

IMPORTANT DATES

  • Submission of Abstract:5th June 2023
  • Acceptance of Abstract:25th June 2023
  • Submission of Full Paper:25th July 2023
  • Start of Registrations:1st August 2023
  • Conference Dates:12th And 13th September 2023

SCHEDULE

THEMES

The research articles are accepted from the following areas of research-

  • Advancements in Structural Engineering and Construction Management.
  • Advancements in Geotechnical Engineering.
  • Advancements in Transportation Engineering.
  • Advancements in Environmental Engineering and water resources Engineering.
  • Advancements in Sustainable Materials .
  • usage of Artificial Intelligence and Computinng Techniques in Civil Engineering.
  • Remote Sensing and GIs Applications in Civil Engineering.

INSTRUCTIONS TO PARTICIPANTS

  • Invited talks will be delivered by experts from academia and industry
  • All accepted papers based on thie merit will be given an opportunity to present orally.
  • All papers are subjected to peer review process and all
  • Accepted papers in this conference will be published.
  • In IOP:Earth and Environmental Sciences(Indexed in SCOPUS and Web of science)
  • All the papers must be free from plagiarism.Similarity index be less than 20%.
  • All papers must be original and not been submitted to any other conferences or journals.
  • Registration of at least one author per paper is mandatory.
  • Registration fee includes access to all technical sessions,participation certificate and publication charges.
  • Authors must present the paper in the given time slot.
  • A one-page abstract not more than 300 words with complete details of authors and affiliations is invited from prospecting authors through the following email:asmi2023@ksrmce.ac.in
